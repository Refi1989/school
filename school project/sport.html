Sport at Reformed




At Reformed Primary we encourage our students to embrace a well-rounded life by providing them with the time and support to pursue sporting activities. Sport and physical activities are structured differently for Foundation phase and Intermediate phase students. At the foundation phase, two hours a week are set aside for physical activities during school time, in addition to optional extracurricular sport activities. At the Intermediate phase, sport is built into the timetable, with students being allowed to choose between sporting and art extracurricular activities, with each being promoted at particular times of the year.Students are encouraged to pursue a variety of sporting interests. This provides the opportunity to teach valuable lessons about teamwork and to develop a healthy attitude towards competition. sporting and art activities avaliable to Reformed students includes hockey,rugby,soccer and art.
